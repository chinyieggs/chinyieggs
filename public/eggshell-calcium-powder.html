Eggshell Calcium Powder

A natural calcium carbonate powder derived from eggshells.

Highly bioavailable calcium source for food fortification and dietary supplements.

Key Benefits

  • Natural calcium carbonate (CaCO₃)
  • High bioavailability
  • Contains trace minerals (Mg, Sr, etc.)
  • Fine particle size for easy mixing
  • Sustainable upcycled ingredient

Tablets and capsules for bone health

Food Fortification

Calcium enrichment for baked goods and beverages

Pet Food

Calcium source for pet nutrition

Agriculture

Soil amendment and fertilizer additive
