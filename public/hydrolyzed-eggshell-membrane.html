Biotech Ingredient Application Series

Our Biotech Ingredient Application Series transforms egg by-products into high-value functional ingredients for use in health supplements, cosmetics, and food enrichment.

By applying advanced bioprocessing techniques, we extract and refine active components from eggshells and eggshell membranes, creating sustainable, naturally derived materials with strong market potential.

All biotech products are manufactured in certified facilities with full traceability and strict quality control.

Hydrolyzed Eggshell Membrane

A water-soluble eggshell membrane powder produced through enzymatic hydrolysis.

Rich in collagen, elastin, and glycosaminoglycans (including hyaluronic acid, chondroitin sulfate, and glucosamine).

Supports joint health, skin elasticity, and connective tissue.

Key Components

  • Collagen (Type I, V, X)
  • Elastin
  • Hyaluronic Acid
  • Chondroitin Sulfate
  • Glucosamine
